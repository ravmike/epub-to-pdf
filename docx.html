EPUB to DOCX for cleaner reader parsing.

This page keeps the conversion fully inside your browser and favors semantic document structure over page layout tricks. The result is meant to be easier for ElevenReader and similar apps to ingest than the PDF path.

What changes

The generated document keeps EPUB headings as real Word headings, adds a Word TOC field, preserves paragraph flow, and avoids forced page breaks between EPUB spine items.

Local only

EPUB unpacking, chapter detection, image embedding, and DOCX assembly all happen client-side.
